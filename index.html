This application transform the geoghraphic coordinates from clarck-1880 datum to the world wgs-1984 datum and vise versa, using the "coordinates frame" method and the 7 parameters(for translation and rotation in 3d dimensions) to achieve the best accuracy needed for geomatics and surveying works.
