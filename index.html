This application transform the coordinates from clarck-1880 datum to the world wgs-1984 datum and vise versa, using the "coordinates frame" method and the 7 parameters(for transition and rotation in 3d dimensions) to achieve the best accuracy needed for geomatics and surveying works in Lebanon and Syria.
